# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 14, 2026

## California Housing Price Predictor

This privacy policy describes how this website collects and uses information.

## Information We Collect

### Analytics Data (via Google Analytics)

This website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects:

- **Geographic Information**: Country, region, city (approximate)
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Usage Information**: Pages visited, time on site, interactions
- **Technical Information**: IP address (anonymized), screen resolution

### What We DON'T Collect

- ❌ No personal identification information (names, emails)
- ❌ No login credentials or passwords
- ❌ No financial information
- ❌ No form data or prediction inputs are sent to any server
- ❌ Your housing price predictions are processed locally in your browser

## How We Use Information

Analytics data is used only to:

1. Understand visitor demographics and geographic distribution
2. Improve website performance and user experience
3. Identify popular features and content
4. Track overall site traffic and trends

## Data Processing

- **All machine learning processing happens in your browser**
- Your prediction inputs never leave your device
- No data from the prediction form is transmitted to any server
- The California Housing dataset is loaded from a public GitHub repository

## Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to:

- Distinguish unique visitors
- Remember your visit
- Track site usage

You can:

- Block cookies in your browser settings
- Use browser extensions to block Google Analytics
- Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request what data is collected about you
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Opt-out**: Block analytics tracking
- **Portability**: Request a copy of your data

### How to Opt-Out:

1. **Browser Settings**: Enable "Do Not Track"
2. **Browser Extensions**: Install privacy extensions like Privacy Badger
3. **Google Analytics Opt-out**: Install [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

## Data Security

- No user accounts or stored credentials
- No backend database to compromise
- All processing is client-side
